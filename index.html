Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our AI website builder.

Is it really free?

Yes! Website generation is completely free. We only offer optional paid hosting services after your website is created, but you're never obligated to use them.

How does the AI work?

Our advanced AI analyzes thousands of website designs and your specific requirements to generate a custom website tailored to your needs. It understands design principles, layout structures, and functionality requirements.

Can I edit the website after generation?

Absolutely! You'll receive all the source files which you can modify as needed. Our AI can also help you make adjustments if you describe what changes you'd like.

What if I don't like the generated website?

You can regenerate as many times as you want until you're satisfied. Each time you generate, the AI learns from your feedback to produce better results.

Do I own the website?

Yes, 100%. The website and all its code belongs to you. We don't claim any rights to your generated content.
